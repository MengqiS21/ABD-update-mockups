Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get reimbursed by my employer? ▼
Yes — many of our students use employer tuition reimbursement. We provide all the documentation your HR department needs. Our advisors can also help you draft the request to your employer.
What if I need to pause my program? ▼
Life happens. You can request one program pause per enrollment. Self-paced students have 12 months from enrollment to complete. Cohort students can transfer to the next available cohort once at no charge.
Is there a refund policy? ▼
We offer a full refund within 7 days of enrollment if you haven't completed more than 10% of the program. After that, we offer a prorated credit toward another program.
Do you offer group rates for small teams? ▼
Yes. Groups of 3+ receive 15% off; groups of 10+ receive up to 40% off with volume pricing. Contact our employer team for a custom quote.
