Frequently asked questions

Is Groundwork really free to read?
Yes — always. We will never put investigations behind a paywall. Reader support enables us to do the work; it doesn't gate access to it.
Is my contribution tax-deductible?
Yes, for U.S. donors. Groundwork Journalism is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. We'll send you a tax receipt via email.
Can I cancel my monthly support?
Yes, at any time, with no penalty. You can manage or cancel your support from your account settings or by emailing support@groundwork.news.
Does Groundwork accept institutional or foundation funding?
We accept a limited number of unrestricted grants from vetted foundations, disclosed publicly in our annual transparency report. We do not accept grants that specify editorial direction.
